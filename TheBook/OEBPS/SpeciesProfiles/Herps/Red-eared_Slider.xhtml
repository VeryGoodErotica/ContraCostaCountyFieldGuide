Reproduction

Turtles can live a very long time, and as they grow, they require larger and larger terrariums. This is a species that is frequently released into the wild when the owner no longer wants to keep it, many populations within Contra Costa County may actually be sustained and growing as a result of released pets rather than through sexual reproduction.

However there is non-conclusive evidence this species is successfully reproducing in the Marsh Creek Drainage: iNaturalist Observation 38784399.
