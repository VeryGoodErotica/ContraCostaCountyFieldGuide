Conservation

The American Bullfrog is an introduced Invasive Species.

Populations are quite healthy in its native range within the south-aastern United States and there iNaturalist records for introduced populations of this species throughout the continental United States, Hawaii, and Mexico with localized records in South America, Asia, and Europe that may indicate growing invasive populations on those continents as well.

In Contra Costa County, it would really be nice if an effective removal solution could be implemented, especially in areas close to extant California Red-legged Frog and Western Pond Turtle populations.
