Taxonomy

NOTE: Traditionally Cormorants (family Phalacrocoracidae) are grouped within Pelecaniformes, however that results in Pelecaniformes being polyphyletic.

To resolve the issue, Cornorants and some relatives have been moved into a new Order Suliformes. However since only a few species are included in this guide, for the time being I went with tradition and placed them with the other Pelecaniformes species.

When this field guide has an actual Avian curator, that very well may change.
