Taxonomy

Traditionally Cormorants (family Phalacrocoracidae) have been grouped within Pelecaniformes, however that results in Pelecaniformes being paraphyletic. Avian taxonomy is still in quite a state of flux.

To resolve the issue, Cormorants and some relatives have been moved into a new Order Suliformes. As only a few species from that order are included in this guide, for the time being I went with tradition and place them here with the Pelecaniformes species.
