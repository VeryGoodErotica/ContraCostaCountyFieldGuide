Creative Writing

Without beloved nature, there is no soul
No ‘Cliff Walk at Pourville’, with waves that roll
All we’d have is Desparation
Lacking Life with Inspiration

With our study we must not forget
The Inspiration that our eyes have met
With the rest of the world, it’s our job to share
In hopes they too will have cause to care

—Michael A. Peters
